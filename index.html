About

Hello, I’m Hasanul Mukit, a dedicated computer engineering graduate with a passion for creating innovative digital experiences. With expertise in web development, UI/UX design, and content writing. I thrive at the intersection of creativity and technology, where I craft solutions that are both functional and visually appealing.

I’m currently building my professional portfolio, dedicating significant time to developing practical projects , all of which reflect my commitment to clean code and user-focused design.

I believe in lifelong learning, which drives me to constantly explore new tools and technologies. My tech stack includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and Tailwind CSS, among others. Beyond coding, I enjoy diving into prototyping, AutoCAD projects, creating detailed and precise designs that mirror my engineering background.

Alongside my core expertise, I actively engage in AI/ML research and development. I’ve collaborated on multiple group projects that leverage machine learning algorithms to solve real-world challenges, and I regularly participate in AI competitions to push the boundaries of innovation. These experiences have deepened my understanding of artificial intelligence and enhanced my ability to develop data-driven solutions.

When I’m not coding or designing, you’ll likely find me exploring ways to incorporate sustainability into technology or enjoying the company of a book. My interests also extend to creating interactive and educational platforms like my project ‘Knowledge Verse,’ which aims to make computer science topics accessible to all.

I’m always looking to connect with like-minded individuals and collaborate on projects that make a difference. If you’re looking for a creative problem solver with a knack for bringing ideas to life, let’s connect!
